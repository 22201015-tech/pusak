Who We Are

A Unified Platform for Change Makers

Public University Students’ Association of Kamalganj (PUSAK) is a non-political, voluntary youth organization formed by current and former students of public universities and medical colleges from Kamalganj Upazila.

We serve as a unified platform for Kamalganj Publicians to contribute their knowledge, leadership, and skills toward social development, education, and community well-being while preserving our rich cultural heritage.

Non-political
Voluntary
Youth-Driven

Our Mission

To contribute to the sustainable development of Kamalganj Upazila through education, social service, awareness campaigns, and career guidance—empowering students to create positive social impact.

Our Vision

To become a leading youth organization that inspires unity, leadership, and positive change—building a future where every student actively contributes to society while preserving Kamalganj’s identity.

Focus Areas

We channel our energy into sectors that create tangible impacts for the people of Kamalganj.

Educational Support

Scholarships, book distribution, and guidance for admission seekers.

Disaster Relief

Immediate response teams for floods and emergency humanitarian aid.

Youth Empowerment

Skill development workshops and leadership training programs.

Social Problem Solving

Community-driven initiatives to address local challenges.

Health Awareness

Medical camps, blood donation drives, and hygiene campaigns.

Cultural Preservation

Celebrating and protecting the rich heritage of Kamalganj.
